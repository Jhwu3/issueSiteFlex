Benefits of Oysters

Oysters are a crucial component of global ocean health. These animals filter and clean the surrounding water and provide habitat, food, and jobs. In some places, oyster reefs can serve as barriers to storms and tides, preventing erosion and protecting productive estuary waters. (from NOAA)

Restoration Progress

Efforts to restore oyster populations are ongoing. With the hopes of one day recovering the water quality and oysters of New York, The Billion Oyster Project is actively working with voluenteers, schools and restaurants to restore one Billion oysters in the waters by 2035!
